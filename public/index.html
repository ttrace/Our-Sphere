Our Sphere

Our Sphere is a portable 3D data-map viewer for mobile and web.

Our Sphere on your web (API)

<iframe class="oursphere"
        src="http://taiyolab.com/oursphere/?map=[your prefered image URL(escaped!)]"
        width=200 height=200>
</iframe>
  • Importing image from Web
  • show orbital element(s)

Our Sphere on iOS

http://lab.taiyolab.com/ourspheredev/
http://taiyolab.com/ourspheredev/?map=[your prefered image URL(escaped!)]
